# Content Plan — Digital & Public

Blog post ideas, roughly ordered. Mix of Spanish and English depending on audience.

## Ready / Near-ready

1. **Legislation as Code: Can We Fix EU Data Law the Way We Fix Software?** (EN)
   — Previously drafted. Cross-post here.

2. **Implementing the Data Act: A Compliance Checklist** (EN)
   — Practical checklist of initiatives organisations must launch to comply with the Data Act by September 2025. Actionable, not theoretical.

## Drafted / In progress

3. **Taskforce de la administracion: 10 proyectos por impacto al ciudadano o ahorro de costes** (ES)
   — The 10 most impactful digital projects Spain's public administration should tackle, ranked by citizen impact and cost savings.

4. **Estonia vs. Spain: Two Models of Electronic Administration** (EN/ES)
   — What Estonia built with X-Road and e-Residency, what Spain has with Cl@ve and SIDE, and why the gap exists. Not a ranking — a structural comparison.

5. **La organizacion TIC del Estado: por que otra forma es posible** (ES)
   — Why Spain's IT governance model (SGAD, scattered CTOs, fragmented procurement) produces the results it does, and what an alternative looks like.

6. **Contratacion publica y reformas: cuanto ahorramos si lo cambiamos?** (ES)
   — Public procurement as the hidden bottleneck. Quantify the cost of the current model and what reforms (frameworks, agile contracts, simplified thresholds) could save.

7. **Servicios predictivos durante todos los procesos vitales del ciudadano** (ES)
   — Proactive government: instead of citizens requesting services, the state anticipates needs at life events (birth, education, employment, retirement, death). What data and systems this requires.

8. **CMS in the Age of AI** (EN)
   — Content management systems were built for human editors. What happens when AI generates, translates, and personalizes content? Do we still need traditional CMS?

9. **Rehacer SIDE con las herramientas actuales: de agentes IA a skills para el evaluador** (ES)
   — Reimagining Spain's public procurement evaluation system (SIDE) using AI agents, LLM-based evaluation skills, and modern architecture. From manual scoring to assisted judgment.

10. **Gestion de la demanda de servicios comunes** (ES)
    — How shared services in government fail: the demand management problem. When every ministry can request anything, nothing gets prioritized. A framework for saying no.

11. **How to Rebuild Public Administration for AI Agents: Forget the UI** (EN)
    — Government digital services were built for humans clicking through forms. AI agents don't need UIs — they need APIs, structured data, and machine-readable policies. What changes when the "user" is an agent.
